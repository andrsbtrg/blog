About me

While studying Bachelor of Architecture at Universidad de Los Andes in Colombia I realized I had a deep interest in understanding structures involved in architectural design. This is when I started a double degree with Civil Engineering, with the idea to understand structures and use that to create more integrated and richer designs. Majoring in engineering made me also found a passion for algorithmic and computational thinking which is why now I am enrolled in a Master program in Computational Design at the university of applied sciences of Ostwestfalen-Lippe in Germany. Here I aim to enrich my knowledge, learning digital fabrication methods combined with advanced form finding to align my two passions and to develop solutions for the new complexities that the world demands of new building projects.
